OpenWriter is an AI writing tool that offers unlimited conversations, messaging, and content generation. It integrates ChatGPT to provide real-time content suggestions and has various templates such as email and article generators, code converters, and language translation. The tool supports Apple Sign In and uses AI and natural language processing. It has a freemium business model, with a premium plan for advanced features and simultaneous content generation. The Dark mode feature is also available for comfortable viewing.
